What is Shareabouts?

Shareabouts is a simple web app for crowdsourced mapping. You can use it to collect public input on maps. People can put dots on the map, add comments, discuss other people's suggestions.

Shareabouts can be used to:

  • gather suggestions for possible locations for bike share stations
  • get comments and votes on proposed locations for food carts
  • collect data on vacant lots that could be urban farms
  • show infrastructure options and get public comment on each one
  • collect support for possible park locations
  • crowdsource issue reporting after a storm

Read more about Shareabouts at shareabouts.org, or head to Shareabouts on github. The project is open source, and we welcome your involvement.
