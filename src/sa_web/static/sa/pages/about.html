About

Hello! You're looking at a demo of Shareabouts, a mapping project from OpenPlans.

In this demo here, we're showing how Shareabouts can be used to gather multiple types of information about a single area.

There is a lot going on in Manhattan's Upper West Side. In order to create a more walkable, livable neighborhood, Community Board 7 (CB7) is collecting information about the place we live in.

Some of the information we're tracking:

  • Schools
  • Sidewalk cracks
  • SROs (Single-Room Occupancy)

Every resident is a local expert! Please use this map to tell us about things you see.

Submit locations you know. With your help, we will all have a better understanding of local probems in the Upper West Side.
