About

Hello! You're looking at a demo of Shareabouts, a mapping project from OpenPlans.

In this demo here, we're showing how Shareabouts can be used to survey the usage of a public space. These analytics can be used to highlight where spaces are working well and where they could be improved, as well as offer insights into who the space is serving and when.

Shareabouts is a simple web app for crowdsourced mapping. You can use it to collect public input on maps - for example, where should a new park be built? Which bus stops need a new shelter? People can put dots on the map, add comments, discuss other people's suggestions and share locations over social networks.

Read more about Shareabouts at shareabouts.org, or head to Shareabouts on github. The project is open source, and we welcome your involvement.

Thanks for trying out Shareabouts!
